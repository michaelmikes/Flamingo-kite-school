INTRODUCTION OF KITE SURFING

Land Introduction Course


  • Learn How to setup a kite launching and landing.
  • Learn How to control a bar and how its works.
  • Learn about the wind and its effects on kiting.
  • Learn how do you choose a safe location.
  • Learn about all safety systems and signs.

Water Introduction Course


  • Learn How to fly a kite Launch and relaunch in the water.
  • Learn about technique to Fly the kite with one hand.
  • Learn How to make a Bodydrugging with two hands.
  • Learn about Safety location in the water.
  • Learn how to ride upwind downwind.
